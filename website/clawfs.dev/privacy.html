Privacy Policy

With BYOC (Bring Your Own Cloud) configurations, your data remains in your chosen cloud environment (AWS, GCP, Azure, Cloudflare, or other supported providers). We do not access, store, or process your file contents unless explicitly required for support purposes with your authorization.

While we implement reasonable security measures, you acknowledge that ClawFS is a tool provided as-is. We are not responsible for any data loss, corruption, or security incidents that may occur in your environment or through the use of our services. You are responsible for maintaining appropriate backups and security controls for your data.

4. Third-Party Services

When using our Partner storage provider option, your data is subject to the third party's privacy and security practices. We recommend reviewing their policies before use.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated metadata at any time by contacting us at hi@clawfs.dev. Data stored in your own cloud accounts must be managed through your cloud provider's tools.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of ClawFS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
