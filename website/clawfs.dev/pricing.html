Pricing

Start Cheap. Keep Storage in Your Bucket. Upgrade Controls When the Fleet Demands It.

The pricing model is simple on purpose. Free gets you a tiny hosted demo path. Pro moves you to bring-your-own storage and real team usage. Enterprise adds governance, cryptography, audit, and operational controls.

Hosted demo storage is included only on the free tier. Pro and Enterprise are built around BYOB so the storage bill stays in your cloud account instead of becoming a hidden platform markup.

Why Free Exists

Teams should be able to feel the product before wiring buckets, IAM, or internal approvals. Free is deliberately small and time-limited so the first experience is one command, not a procurement process.

Why Pro Is BYOB

Once you move past a demo, the cleanest model is to keep object storage in your environment. That keeps cloud permissions straightforward and avoids turning ClawFS into a storage reseller.

Start on free. Move to Pro when the volumes matter. Add Enterprise when policy shows up.

The product story stays simple even as the controls get more serious.
