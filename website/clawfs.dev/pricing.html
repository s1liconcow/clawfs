Pricing

Start Fast. Keep Storage in Your Environment. Add Controls as the Team Grows.

The pricing model is designed to make evaluation easy and production predictable. Start with a hosted trial, move to bring-your-own storage for real workloads, and add enterprise controls when platform requirements show up.

Pro and Enterprise are built around BYOC (Bring Your Own Cloud) or Partner storage so the data stays in your cloud account or our trusted provider, keeping the infrastructure bill visible, predictable, and under your control.

Why Free Exists

Teams should be able to evaluate ClawFS before wiring buckets, IAM, or security reviews. Free is sized to make that first experience quick and concrete rather than turning it into a procurement exercise.

Why Pro Is BYOC

Own your own data — ClawFS can offer a seamless experience via our partner storage provider for a small fee, but we find many teams would prefer to use their own cloud's storage services. Both are supported.
